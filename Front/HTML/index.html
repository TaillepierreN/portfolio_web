Chrauma

Our journey culminates in this final project, a testament to our growth, collaboration, and dedication throughout our academic endeavor. Join us Nicolas, Thomas and Colas students at Holberton School in Paris, France.

Immerse yourself in our creation, a dynamic video game sculpted with the versatile tools of Unity. For Nicolas and Thomas, this project serves as a gateway to their shared vision of specializing in the dynamic fields of AR/VR where innovation meets imagination. Meanwhile, Colas charts his course towards Full-Stack mastery, finding his niche within the intricate web of development possibilities.

Our decision to embark on this ambitious undertaking stems from a deep-rooted passion for gaming and a relentless pursuit of knowledge. Beyond fulfilling academic requirements, this project embodies our collective desire to push boundaries, explore new horizons, and leave a lasting mark on the ever-evolving landscape of technology. As we stand on the precipice of this milestone, we invite you to join us in celebrating not just the culmination of a year's worth of hard work but also the beginning of a new chapter in our educational odyssey.

For the music we wanted a fairly dark direction, with influences like DOOM, artists working in bass music were interested in this project

The Iridium project was born at the end of 2O16, these French DJs / Producers
have set up this project with the aim of propagating a new form of brutal hardcore, signed by The Third Movement and Heresy.
Influenced by Electronic Music in general, they mix Hardcore and violence with Drum and Bass, Crossbreed, Dubstep, Trap, Hardtechno... Metal culture is also omnipresent, in order to add power to the sound thanks to the guitar riffs and drum patterns.

Nagazaki is a French DJ/Composer
He mainly composes at 175bpm, influenced by Breakcore, Dubstep, Schranz and Neurofunk. He is signed to The Third Movement and Heresy. His style is a mix between powerful hardcore kicks with lots of breakbeats, kick variations, and vocals from video games, films, series, and poetry.
